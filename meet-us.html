A family business

We've been friends for over twenty years , sharing a passion for technology that has only grown stronger over time. Over the last decade , we've honed our skills into something remarkable. Together, we've built an impressive array of computers - each a testament to our skilled craftmanship.

We've both specialised in small form factor PCs - sleek, compact, lightweight , without any compromise in performance.

From supercharged gaming PCs , where victory can be decided by a single frame, to specialist home music studio workstations , where silent operation matters just as much as high performance, we've navigated the world of computers.
But it doesn't stop there - our expertise extends to the enterprise world of specialised workloads for engineering and architecture design, whether you're simulating the effect of cross-wind on your latest supercar, or showcasing the intricate 3D renders of your client's latest office design.

We are ready to turn your tech dreams into reality. Whether it's a sleek workstation to compose your next hit, or a powerful gaming rig that you can pop into your backpack and take to uni, we don't just defy limits.
We architect the impossible.
