Client builds

Machine Learning PC for Student

For his masters thesis, our client was developing a model to predict the outcomes of horse-races.He came to us looking to speed up his model training time, compared to his Dell XPS laptop.  He also needed the PC to fit in a suitcase for short haul flights or trains to Scotland.We built a water-cooled M-atx system. Selecting a CPU with high single-threaded performance to speed up Python Scripts, and an 8GB RTX 3070 for training on large datasets.

Backpack Gaming PC

We crammed an 8-core Ryzen 7 5800X3D and top of the range AMD Radeon 7900 XTX into a 10 litre case.
Our client is now able to take his gaming PC in his backpack between cities when he visits his parents.He uses his PC with both 4K 60Hz and 1440p 165hz monitors.

Professional Audio Worksation

Our client's laptop was struggling with a large number of audio channels in Ableton Live 12.Complete silence was required, since recording would often be done in the same room as the computer.Backpack PCs provided a solution featuring an 8-core Ryxen 7 3700X for strong multithreaded performance, and an affordable refurbished GTX 970 GPU. Both CPU and GPU were very power efficient, enabling the fans to be switched off completely when recording.

RGB Gaming PC

Our client was looking for high end specs but also a PC to match the rest of his setup. We provided an RGB case with customisable coloured lighting, a vertical GPU mount, water-cooling for his CPU, and an RTX 3080. The lighting can then be adjusted to match his desk lighting.
