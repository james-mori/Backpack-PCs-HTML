FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Common questions

Here are some of the most common questions that we get.
How does the warranty work? Our PCs are vigorously tested. In the unlikely situation where it does stop working, send us a video or description of your issue. If it needs repair, we'll pay for shipping back to us, fix it, and ship back to you. We'll aim to do this in one week. Extend the warranty by another year at any time during the first year
I'm not really sure what sort of computer I need. Are you still able to help Absolutely! Just let us know what its for, and we'll be able to guide you on the absolute best specifications you can get for your budget
What are the advantages of a backpack-sized computer? A backpack-sized computer offers portability and convenience. It allows you to easily carry your powerful computer wherever you go, whether it's for work, gaming, or other purposes.
Why not just get a gaming laptop? Gaming laptop definitely have their use. But a desktop provides typically about double the performance for the price. They can also be upgraded extending the life of your PC by 5+ years. The fastest desktop will also always be quieter and more powerful than the fastest gaming laptop available due to better cooling and power delivery
How do I request a quote? To request a quote, simply click on the 'Request Quote' button on our website. Fill out the required information, including your desired specifications, and our team will get back to you with a personalized quote.
